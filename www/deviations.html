Tips for Mastering Deviations

  • Start with Insurance (+3) and Hard 16 vs 10 (+0) – they’re the most valuable
  • Memorize the Illustrious 18 first – they capture ~80% of the gain from all possible indexes
  • Fab 4 only matters when late surrender is offered (common in shoe games)
  • Use the in-app Hi-Lo trainer – it will tell you when you should have deviated
  • Practice at low counts first to solidify basic strategy, then add deviations at high counts
  • Accuracy matters more than speed – a perfect count with these 22 indexes beats sloppy full indexes

Practice daily — your edge will grow fast!
