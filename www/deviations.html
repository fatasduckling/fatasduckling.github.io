Index Plays & Deviations (Illustrious 18 + Fab 4)

These are the most valuable changes from basic strategy when the True Count is high enough. Mastering them adds a huge edge when card counting.

Color Legend – What Each Color Means:

Green – Stand instead of Hit
Blue – Double instead of Hit
Red – Surrender instead of Hit
Orange – Take Insurance

How to Use These Tables

The number in each cell is the True Count threshold. When the True Count is equal to or higher than this number, make the colored play instead of basic strategy.

Blank cells = No valuable deviation – always follow basic strategy.

These indexes are for Hi-Lo counting in multi-deck games with typical rules.

Tips for Learning & Using Deviations

  • Memorize the Illustrious 18 first – they give ~80% of the counting edge from indexes
  • Fab 4 only applies when late surrender is offered (common in multi-deck shoe games)
  • Insurance at +3 is the single most valuable play – never miss it!
  • Use the full counting trainer in this app – it gives feedback when you should have deviated
  • Start slow: only use the top 5–10 indexes until comfortable
  • Deviations only make sense when you’re accurately keeping the True Count

Practice these consistently and watch your advantage grow!
